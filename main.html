HOME

Welcome to your student Locker home page.

This is where you'll find general information and updates.
This is the place where you can access all the resources and services that you need as a student of our institution. Whether you want to check your activity points, enroll in courses, upload certificates or communicate with your instructors and mentors, you can do it all here.

How to use the Student Locker
To use the Student Portal, you need to log in with your student ID and password. If you don't have an account yet, you can create one by clicking on the "Register" button. Once you are logged in, you can navigate through the different sections of the portal using the menu bar at the top of the page.
